01 — Brand & Developer Heritage
Who We Are
Dream House Constructions is a premier real estate developer in the Philippines, known for integrating sustainable "green" technology with modern urban luxury. Our portfolio spans from high-end corporate towers in Makati to resort-inspired residences in Cebu and Davao.
We focus on "Passive Cooling" architecture (like our Amihan Breezeways) and smart-home integration. Every project we build must meet a minimum of a "Green Building" certification to ensure long-term value and environmental harmony.

03 — Payment & Financing
Flexible Terms
We offer flexible structures. Our most popular is the 10/10/80 scheme: 10% spot downpayment, 10% equity spread over the construction period (0% interest), and 80% balance through bank financing or lumpsum.
Yes, we offer in-house financing for up to 10 years, though we highly recommend bank financing for more competitive interest rates.
04 — Building Bye-Laws & Construction Guidelines
Rules & Regulations
Absolutely. All our structures are designed and built in strict accordance with PD 1096 (National Building Code) and RA 9514 (Fire Code). We also adhere to local zoning ordinances regarding Floor Area Ratio (FAR) and setbacks.
To maintain aesthetic integrity and structural safety, exterior modifications are strictly prohibited under the Deed of Restrictions. This includes changing window types or painting balconies.
Before starting any interior work, owners must submit a Fit-Out Plan to the Building Administration. This ensures your contractor adheres to weight limits, plumbing layouts, and electrical load capacities defined in the building's technical specifications.
For our House and Lot developments, solar installations are encouraged but must follow the neighborhood's height and placement guidelines to avoid obstructing the views or sunlight of neighboring properties.

Additional slots are subject to availability and are usually offered first to owners of 3-bedroom units and Penthouses.

07 — Green Parks
Gardens & Green Spaces
Our professional Landscaping Team maintains all common green areas daily, covered by your monthly Association Dues. We use organic fertilizers and recycled rainwater to keep our parks vibrant.
While common areas are managed by the developer, we have designated "Community Plots" in projects like Luntian Terraces where residents can adopt a small patch for personal gardening.
Every first Saturday of the quarter, we host "Green Days" where residents are invited to join our horticulturists for workshops on urban gardening and sustainable living. It's a great way to meet your neighbors!

09 — Post-Sales & Turnover
Move-In Ready
Once your unit is ready, you will receive a Notice of Acceptance. You will have 30 days to inspect the unit. Any "snagging" items (minor repairs) will be addressed by our team before you officially move in.
We provide a 1-year warranty on workmanship (leaks, electrical, etc.) and a 15-year warranty on the building's structural integrity.

11 — Community Policies
Living Together
Most of our residential brands are pet-friendly, allowing up to two small-to-medium-sized pets per unit. Residents must adhere to the "Pet Manifesto" regarding noise and common area cleanliness.
