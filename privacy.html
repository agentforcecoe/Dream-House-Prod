At Dream House Premium Real Estate, your privacy is fundamental to how we operate. This Privacy Notice explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, how we use and protect it, and what rights you have under the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173). We encourage you to read this Notice carefully.

01

Overview

This Privacy Notice applies to all personal data collected by Dream House Premium Real Estate, Inc. ("Dream House", "we", "us", or "our") through:

  • Our website and any subdomains (the "Site")
  • Property enquiry forms and contact submissions
  • Email, telephone, and in-person consultations with our agents
  • Social media channels and digital marketing platforms
  • In-office visits to any Dream House branch or showroom

By using our Site or engaging with our services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Notice. Where we rely on your consent to process your data, you may withdraw that consent at any time.

03

Data We Collect

Depending on how you interact with us, we may collect the following categories of personal data:

Category Examples
Identity Data Full name, date of birth, nationality, government-issued ID details
Contact Data Email address, mobile number, residential or office address
Property Enquiry Data Preferred property type, location, budget range, intended use (personal/investment), financing preferences
Financial Data General income bracket, payment method preference (for financing assessment only — no full financial data is stored)
Technical Data IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, session duration, referring URL
Communications Data Records of emails, enquiries, chat messages, or call notes from interactions with our agents
Marketing Preferences Consent status, preferred communication channel, property alert subscriptions

We do not collect sensitive personal information (such as health data, religious beliefs, or biometric data) unless required by law or explicitly consented to by you.

04

How We Collect Your Data

We collect personal data through the following means:

  • Direct interactions — when you fill out enquiry forms, subscribe to property alerts, call our office, or visit our showroom
  • Automated technologies — through cookies, web analytics tools (such as Google Analytics), and server logs when you browse the Site
  • Third parties — from property developers, referral partners, and social media platforms (e.g. Facebook Lead Ads or Instagram enquiry forms) where you have engaged with our advertisements
  • Publicly available sources — limited professional information available on public registers or business directories for corporate client due diligence
05

Purpose of Processing

We process your personal data for the following purposes:

  • Responding to your property enquiries and providing brokerage services
  • Matching you with suitable property listings based on your stated preferences
  • Scheduling property viewings, site visits, and consultations with our agents
  • Processing reservation agreements and coordinating with developers on your behalf
  • Sending you property alerts, new listing announcements, and market updates (with your consent)
  • Complying with legal and regulatory obligations, including Anti-Money Laundering (AMLA) know-your-client (KYC) requirements
  • Improving the functionality and content of our Site through analytics
  • Managing our business operations, client records, and agent commissions
  • Protecting the security of our Site and preventing fraudulent activity
06

Legal Basis for Processing

Under the Data Privacy Act of 2012, we process your personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Consent — for marketing communications, property alerts, and optional data sharing with developer partners
  • Contract — when processing is necessary to carry out our brokerage services at your request
  • Legal obligation — to comply with KYC, AMLA, and other regulatory requirements imposed on licensed real estate brokerages in the Philippines
  • Legitimate interests — for site analytics, security monitoring, and improving our services, where these interests are not overridden by your privacy rights

Your right to withdraw consent: Where we rely on consent, you may withdraw it at any time by contacting us or clicking "Unsubscribe" in any marketing email. Withdrawal does not affect processing carried out before withdrawal.

07

Sharing Your Data

We may share your personal data with the following categories of recipients, only to the extent necessary for the purposes described in this Notice:

  • Property Developers & Sellers — to facilitate reservation or purchase of a specific property you have enquired about
  • Service Providers — trusted third-party vendors who provide IT infrastructure, CRM platforms, email delivery, and analytics tools under strict data processing agreements
  • Regulatory & Government Authorities — including the National Privacy Commission, Bureau of Internal Revenue, Anti-Money Laundering Council, and the Professional Regulation Commission, as required by law
  • Legal & Professional Advisers — where necessary to defend or enforce our legal rights

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party for their independent marketing purposes. Any data sharing with developer partners is subject to your explicit consent.

08

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary for the purpose it was collected or as required by law:

  • Enquiry & consultation records — retained for 5 years from the date of last interaction
  • Client transaction records — retained for 10 years from transaction completion, as required by AMLA regulations
  • Marketing consent records — retained until consent is withdrawn plus 2 years thereafter
  • Website analytics data — retained for 26 months in anonymised or aggregated form
  • CCTV footage (if applicable in our offices) — retained for 30 days and then securely deleted

After the applicable retention period, your personal data is securely deleted or anonymised so that it can no longer be linked to you.

09

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational security measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our security measures include:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmitted through the Site
  • Access controls and role-based permissions for our internal systems
  • Regular security assessments and vulnerability scanning
  • Staff training on data privacy obligations under the Data Privacy Act
  • Secure storage of physical documents and records
  • Incident response procedures compliant with NPC Circular 16-03 (Personal Data Breach Management)

In the event of a personal data breach that is likely to result in a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify the National Privacy Commission within 72 hours and notify affected individuals without undue delay, as required by law.

10

Your Rights

Under the Data Privacy Act of 2012, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Right to be Informed

You have the right to know what personal data we hold about you and how it is being processed.

Right to Access

You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you, including the sources and purposes of processing.

Right to Rectification

You may request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete personal data we hold about you.

Right to Erasure

You may request deletion of your personal data where there is no longer a lawful basis for its continued processing.

Right to Object

You may object to the processing of your personal data for direct marketing or where processing is based on legitimate interests.

Right to Data Portability

You may request a copy of your data in a structured, machine-readable format where technically feasible.

Right to File a Complaint

You have the right to lodge a complaint with the National Privacy Commission (www.privacy.gov.ph) if you believe your rights have been violated.

Right to Damages

You are entitled to claim compensation for any damages suffered as a result of inaccurate, incomplete, or unlawfully obtained personal data.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@dreamhouse.ph. We will respond within 15 business days of receiving your request. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

11

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our Site uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and gather analytics data. The types of cookies we use include:

  • Essential cookies — necessary for the Site to function properly (e.g. session management). These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies — used to understand how visitors interact with the Site (via Google Analytics). These are anonymised and aggregated.
  • Marketing cookies — used to deliver relevant property advertisements on social media and third-party platforms (e.g. Facebook Pixel). These are only set with your consent.
  • Preference cookies — used to remember your settings and preferences across visits.

You can manage or withdraw consent for non-essential cookies at any time through the cookie settings banner on the Site, or by adjusting your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of the Site.

12

International Data Transfers

Some of our third-party service providers (such as cloud infrastructure or email delivery platforms) may process your data outside the Philippines. Where such transfers occur, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including:

  • Data processing agreements incorporating NPC-compliant standard contractual clauses
  • Transfers only to countries or organisations with adequate data protection standards
  • Prior notification to and, where required, approval from the National Privacy Commission
13

Minors

Our services are not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal data from a person under 18 without verified parental consent, we will take immediate steps to delete such data.

If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data without your consent, please contact our Data Protection Officer immediately.

14

Changes to This Privacy Notice

We may update this Privacy Notice from time to time to reflect changes in our data practices, legal requirements, or business operations. When we make material changes, we will:

  • Post the updated Notice on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date
  • Notify registered users by email at least 14 days before the changes take effect
  • Display a prominent banner on the Site for a period of 30 days following the update

We encourage you to review this page periodically. Your continued use of our services after the effective date of any update constitutes acceptance of the revised Notice.
